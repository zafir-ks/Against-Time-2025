Competition Format

  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Format:
    • programming problems blending coding agility and mathematical logic.
    • Questions deactivate after 5 contestants answer correctly
  • Judging criteria:
    • 1. The contestant who solves the maximum number of problem wins
    • 2. In the case of a tie the contestant who took the least number of unsuccessful attempts wins
    • 3. In case the tie persists, the contestant who took the least time wins.
  • Prize :
    • 1. First prize: Gold medal + Laptop bag
    • 2. Second prize: Silver medal + Laptop bag
    • 3. Second prize: Bronze medal + Laptop bag
    • 4. Fourth prize: Laptop bag
